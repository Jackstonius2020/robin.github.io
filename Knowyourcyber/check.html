Find out if your email address or password has appeared in a known data breach. Take action before it is too late.

Have You Been Compromised?

Data breaches happen when attackers gain unauthorised access to a company's database and steal user information. Your email address or password may have been exposed without you even knowing.

Use the tools below to check if your details have appeared in any known breaches recorded in our database. If they have, we strongly recommend changing your password immediately and enabling two-factor authentication.

Is This Safe to Use?

Yes. This tool runs entirely in your browser. Your email address and password are never sent to any server or stored anywhere. Everything is checked locally on your device. We recommend never entering your real password on any website — use this tool to check the strength of a test password instead.
