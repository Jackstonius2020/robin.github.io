About KnowYourCyber

Learn about who we are, what we do, and why cyber awareness matters to us.

Who We Are

KnowYourCyber is a charitable organisation with a simple but important mission: to help everyday people understand and protect themselves from cyber threats.

We were founded by a group of cybersecurity professionals who noticed that while cyber attacks were becoming more common and more damaging, most people had very little knowledge of how to protect themselves. We believe that knowledge is the most powerful defence against cybercrime.

What We Do

Over the years we have compiled a detailed database of cyber threats, data breaches, and attack methods. Our team works continuously to keep this database up to date and to present the information in a way that is clear and accessible to everyone, regardless of their technical background.

Through this website we aim to provide three key services to the public:

Education

Our threats database explains the most common and dangerous cyber attacks in plain language, helping people understand what they are up against.

Awareness

Our breach checker allows individuals to see if their personal data has been exposed, so they can take action before any damage is done.

Prevention

Our protection tips give practical, actionable advice that anyone can follow to significantly reduce their risk of becoming a victim of cybercrime.

Our Values

Accessibility

Cybersecurity should not be something only technical experts understand. We write all our content in plain, clear language so that anyone can benefit from it.

Transparency

We are open about where our data comes from and how we use it. We never collect personal information from users of this website without their knowledge and consent.

Accuracy

All information in our database is carefully researched and regularly reviewed by our team of cybersecurity professionals to ensure it remains accurate and up to date.

Independence

As a charitable organisation we are not funded by any commercial company. This means our advice is always impartial and in the best interests of the public.
