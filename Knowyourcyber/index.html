What is KnowYourCyber?

KnowYourCyber is a charitable organisation dedicated to increasing public awareness of cyber threats. We believe that everyone deserves to understand the risks they face online, regardless of their technical background.

Over the years we have compiled a database of the most common and dangerous cyber threats. Our goal is to present this information in a clear, accessible way so that anyone can learn from it and protect themselves.

Cyber Threats Are Real

Cyber attacks happen every day, targeting individuals, businesses, and organisations of all sizes. Here is a glimpse into the world of cybersecurity.

Why Does This Matter?

Cyber attacks do not only target large companies. Individuals, schools, charities, and small businesses are all at risk. A single phishing email or weak password can lead to identity theft, financial loss, or worse.

The good news is that most cyber attacks are preventable. With the right knowledge, you can significantly reduce your risk. That is exactly what KnowYourCyber is here to help with.
